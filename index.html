Top three activities to do in Bali

Kintamani Mountain

Kintamani mountain is a great place to see the sunrise. Enjoy the view of the mountain in nearest Kintamani villa, cafe or coffee shops

Kuta Beach

Kuta Beach is a great place to see the sunset. Enjoy the view of the beautiful sunset view in nearest resort, cafe or beach club.

Ubud Rice Field

Ubud Rice Field is a great place for walk in the morning or evening. Enjoy the view of Bali famous rice field with a very fresh air.
